## Research Interests

My methodological interests center on developing and applying rigorous statistical tools for observational and clinical data:

- **Causal inference** — treatment effect estimation, propensity score methods, instrumental variables, sensitivity analysis for unmeasured confounding
- **Survival and time-to-event analysis** — competing risks, multi-state models, time-varying covariates
- **Longitudinal data analysis** — linear and generalized mixed models, GEE, missing data mechanisms
- **High-dimensional inference** — penalized regression, variable selection in genomic and EHR settings
- **Statistical methods for public health** — clinical trial design, electronic health records, precision medicine
